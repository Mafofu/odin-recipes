Classic "Krautflecken"

Delicous Krautflecken original Recipe straight out of Heubach

Absolute Delicous.

Ingredients

  • Cabbage
  • Butter
  • "Flecken Pasta"
  • salt, pepper, optional sugar
  • Love

Instructions

  1. Cut cabbage add salt and knead it. Preheat oven to 180 degrees Celsius.
  2. Put it in a baking dish, add butter and bake it for around 20 minutes.
  3. Cook "Flecken Pasta" in salted water until done. Add cabbage and spice it up with salt and pepper or optional sugar
