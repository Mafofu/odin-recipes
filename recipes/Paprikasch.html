Classic Chicken Paprikasch

Delicous Chicken Paprikasch original Recipe straight out of Heubach

Absolute Delicous.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • tomato paste
  • Oil
  • Paprika powder
  • cream
  • salt, pepper, spices
  • Love

Instructions

  1. Cut Onions, cook cuted onions and chicken. Add tomate paste garlic, paprika powder and spices
  2. Add warm water, cook meat until done. Put Chicken aside
  3. Add cream to the sauce, mix it. Add "Maggi" and Worcestershire sauce. As side dish i recommend pasta
